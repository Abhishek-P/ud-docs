Polarity

In Tswana [Polarity]() is only indicated for negative values as `Neg` and applies to morphemes tagged [ADV]() in UPOS and MNEG in XPOS.

### <a name="Neg">`Neg`</a>: negative

#### Examples

* _<b>nepřišel</b>&nbsp;_ “he did not come”
