A locative modifier is a subtype of the [obl]() relation: if the modifier is specifying a location, it is labeled as `lmod`.

~~~ sdparse
Peter le Mary ba ne ba tlamparelana mme ba tswa mo phaposing . \n Peter and Mary hugged each other and then left the room .
obl:lmod(tswa, phaposing)
obl:lmod(left, room[locative])
~~~
