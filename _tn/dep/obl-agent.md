The relation `obl:agent` is used for agents of passive verbs.

~~~ sdparse
Ga ba na kakanyo epe gore e kwadilwe ke mang . \n They have no idea who wrote it .
obl:agent(kwadilwe, mang)
obl:agent(wrote, who)
~~~
