### Definition

Adjectives are words that typically modify nouns and specify their properties or attributes.
They may also function as predicates, as in

- _<b>кызыл</b> машина_ "(the) red car"  
- _Машина <b>кызыл</b>_  "(the) car is red."

Tatar adjectives have an inflected form for a comparative `Degree`, but not for a superlative,
which is specified by the [adverb](ADV) _иң_.

- _<b>тизрәк</b>_ "faster"
- _<b>иң</b> тиз_ "fastest"
`Degree` is specified using [adverbs](ADV) _daha_ and _en_.

We distinguish between adjectives, [determiners](DET) and [numerals](NUM).

### Examples

- _зур_ "big"
- _иске_ "old"
- _яшел_ "green"
- _кадерле_ "dear"
- _милли_ "national"
