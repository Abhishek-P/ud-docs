An adjectival modifier is any adjective that serves to modify the meaning of a nominal head. In Georgian, adjectival modifiers can occur both in pre- and post-nominal position. Adjectives can be found as modifiers of nouns, pronouns, adverbs and other adjectives. E.g.

~~~ sdparse
საბძლიდან გამოვიდა მაღალ-მაღალი კაცი/NOUN. /n 'A tall man came out of the barn.'
amod(კაცი, მაღალ-მაღალი)
~~~
