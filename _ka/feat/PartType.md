The `PartType` feature typically applies to [particles](_ka/pos/PART) and to [adverbs](_ka/pos/ADV).

### <a name="Int">`Int`</a>: question particle

#### Examples

* _ნუთუ_ 'whether', _იქნებ_ 'maybe' etc.

### <a name="Inf">`Inf`</a>: infinitive particle

#### Examples

* _რომელი<b>მე</b>_ 'any', _ვინ<b>მე</b>_ 'anyone' etc.

### <a name="Neg">`Neg`</a>: negative particle

#### Examples

* _არ_ 'not', _ვერ_ 'not' etc.

### <a name="Emp">`Emp`</a>: emphasis particle

#### Examples

* _აკი_ 'but', _მაშ_ 'surely' etc.
