`Number` is an inflectional feature of [nouns](_ka/pos/NOUN), [proper nouns](_ka/pos/PROPN), [pronouns](_ka/pos/PRON),[adjectives](_ka/pos/ADJ), [verbs](_ka/pos/VERB) and very rarely [numerals](_ka/pos/NUM).

### <a name="Sing">`Sing`</a>: singular number

A singular noun denotes one person, animal or thing.

#### Examples

* _სახლი_ "house", _ვწერ_ "I write" etc.

### <a name="Plur">`Plur`</a>: plural number

A plural noun denotes several persons, animals or things.

#### Examples

* _სახლ<b>ებ</b>ი_ "houses", _სახლ<b>ნ</b>ი_ "houses", _ვწერ<b>თ</b>_ "we write" etc.
