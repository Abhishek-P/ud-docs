The `VerbType` feature applies to [verbs](_ka/pos/VERB).

### <a name="Aux">`Aux`</a>: auxiliary verb attached to the main verb

#### Examples

* _ვწერი<b>ვარ</b>_ 'I am written here', _დაუხატი<b>ხარ</b>_ 'probably, you have been drawn by smb.' etc.
