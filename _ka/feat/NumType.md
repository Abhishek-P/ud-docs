The `NumType` feature applies to [numerals](_ka/pos/NUM).

### <a name="Card">`Card`</a>: cardinal number or corresponding word

#### Examples

* _ერთი_ 'one', _ორი_ 'two', 1 'one' etc.

### <a name="Ord">`Ord`</a>: ordinal number or corresponding word

#### Examples

* _პირველი_ 'the first', _მეორე_ 'the second', I 'the first' etc.

### <a name="Frac">`Frac`</a>: fractional number or corresponding word

#### Examples

* _მეორედი_ 'half', _მესამედი_ 'a third part', 1/3 'a third part' etc.

### <a name="Mult">`Mult`</a>: multiple number or corresponding word

#### Examples

* _ორჯერ_ 'twice', _სამჯერ_ 'three times' etc.
