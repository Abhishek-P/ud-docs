The `AdpType` feature typically applies to [adpositions](_ka/pos/ADV), nouns [NOUN](_ka/pos/NOUN), proper nouns [PROPN](_ka/pos/PROPN), numerals  [NUM](_ka/pos/NUM), adjectives [ADJ](_ka/pos/ADJ) and pronouns [PRON](_ka/pos/PRON).

### <a name="Post">`Post`</a>: postposition

#### Examples

* _მაგიდა<b>ზე</b>_ 'on the table', _ჩანთა<b>ში</b>_ 'in the bag' etc.
