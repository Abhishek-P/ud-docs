An auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause.

~~~ sdparse
Aș vrea o portocală . \n Would-I want an orange .
aux(vrea, Aș)
~~~

Exception: The auxiliary verb used to construct the passive voice is not labeled 'aux', but 'auxpass'.
