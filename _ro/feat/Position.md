Certain Romanian determiners can be used only prenominally (before the noun) or only postnominally (after the noun). If there is
a pair of determiners, one pre- and one postnominal, their morphological relation is treated
as derivational, that is, they have different lemmas.

### <a name="Prenom">`Prenom`</a>: prenominal position

#### Examples

* _<b>acest</b>_ “this” (masculine singular)
* _<b>această</b>_ “this” (feminine singular)
* _<b>acești</b>_ “these” (masculine plural)
* _<b>aceste</b>_ “these” (feminine plural)

### <a name="Postnom">`Postnom`</a>: postnominal position

#### Examples

* _<b>acesta, ăsta</b>_ “this” (masculine singular)
* _<b>asta</b>_ “this” (feminine singular)
* _<b>ăștia</b>_ “these” (masculine plural)
* _<b>astea</b>_ “these” (feminine plural)
