### <a name="Nom">`Nom`</a>: nominative

#### Examples

* _<b>sal</b>_ “year, years”


### <a name="Acc">`Acc`</a>: oblique

#### Examples

* _<b>salê</b>_ “year”
* _<b>salan</b>_ “years”


### <a name="Con">`Con`</a>: construct

This case marks possession on the possessed noun, not on the possessor, hence it is not the genitive.
The construct suffix is sometimes also called **ezafe**.

#### Examples

* _<b>sala</b> 1940'ê_ “year of 1940”
* _<b>salên</b>_ “years of”


### <a name="Voc">`Voc`</a>: vocative

#### Examples

* _<b>salê</b>_ “year”
* _<b>saline</b>_ “years”
