Polite is used in Polish as a nominal feature, with the language-specific value `Depr` in case
of special derogatory forms of some human masculine nouns, e.g., _profesory_ “professors (derogatory)”,
as opposed to _profesorowie_ “professors (neutral)”. The neutral forms are not specifically annotated.

### <a name="Depr">`Depr`</a>: derogatory form

#### Examples

* [pl] _<b>profesory</b>_ “professors”
