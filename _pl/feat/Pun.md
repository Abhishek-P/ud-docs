Some abbreviations have to be followed by a period while others do not have to, or even
must not be marked by a period. This feature indicates whether or not the period is expected
(note that the Polish treebanks use a tokenization schema where the period is a separate
token).

### <a name="No">`No`</a>: no period

#### Examples

* _do 221 <b>mld</b> dolarów_ “up to $ 221 billion”

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: there is a period

#### Examples

* _5 <b>tys</b>. dolarów_ “5 thousand dollars”
