### <a name="Dict">`Dict`</a>: the -ṇe form

Used as the citation form of verbs. It may function as a verbal noun and take case suffixes.

#### Examples

* <b>होणे</b> / _<b>hoṇe</b>_ “to be”

### <a name="Incp">`Incp`</a>: the -ū form

#### Examples

* <b>होऊ</b> / _<b>hoū</b>_ “to be”
