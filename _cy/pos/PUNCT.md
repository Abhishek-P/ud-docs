#### Description

Punctuation marks are non-alphabetical characters and character groups used to delimit linguistic units in printed text.

#### Examples

* Comma: _,_
* Period: _._
* Question Mark: _?_
* Exclamation Mark: _!_
* Quote Marks: _" "_
* Parenthesis: _( )_
