Boolean feature of pronouns.  It tells
whether the word is a possessive pronoun. 


### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: it is possessive

Note that there is no `No` value. If the word is not possessive, the
`Poss` feature will just not be mentioned in the `FEAT` column.

#### Examples

- _εμένα, μου_ "my"
- εσένα, σου_ "your"
- _του_ "his", _της_ "her", _του_ "its"
- _του οποίου_  "whose"
