Many tagsets have just one tag for punctuation. Others classify punctuation in
more detail.

### <a name="Peri">`Peri`</a>: period at the end of sentence or clause

#### Examples

*  _Γιατί?, διερωτήθηκε<b>.</b>_ “Why?, she wondered.”

### <a name="Elip">`Elip`</a>: ellipsis

#### Examples

* _Δεν ξέρω <b>…</b>_ “I don't know ...”

### <a name="Qest">`Qest`</a>: question mark

#### Examples

*  _Γιατί<b>?</b>_ “Why?”

### <a name="Excl">`Excl`</a>: exclamation mark

#### Examples

*  _Πρόσεχε<b>!</b>_ “Watch out!”

### <a name="Quot">`Quot`</a>: quotation marks (various sorts in various languages)

#### Examples

* _Είναι, λέει,  <b>"</b>σκεπτική<b>"</b>_ “She is, she says, "concerned"”

### <a name="Brck">`Brck`</a>: bracket

#### Examples

*  _Δημόσια Επιχείρηση Ηλεκτρισμού <b>(</b>ΔΕΗ<b>)</b>_ “Public Electricity Company (PEC)”

### <a name="Comm">`Comm`</a>: comma

#### Examples

*  _Είναι<b>,</b> λέει<b>,</b>  "σκεπτική"_ “She is, she says, "concerned"”


### <a name="Colo">`Colo`</a>: colon

#### Examples

* _Οι εξής<b>:</b>_ “The following:”


### <a name="Dash">`Dash`</a>: dash, hyphen

#### Examples

* _άτομα ηλικίας 25<b>-</b>45 ετών_ “individuals with age between 25 and 45 years”

### <a name="Slsh">`Slsh`</a>: slash or backslash

#### Examples

* _Αθήνα<b>/</b>Αττική_ "Athens/Attica"
