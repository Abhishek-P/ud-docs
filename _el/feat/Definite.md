In Modern Greek, definiteness is  a feature of 
[articles](el-pos/DET). Its value distinguishes whether we are talking about
something known and concrete, or something general or unknown. 

### <a name="Ind">`Ind`</a>: indefinite

#### Examples

- _ένας σκύλος_ "_a_ dog"
- _μία γάτα_ "_a_ cat"
- _ένα πουλί_ "_a_ bird"



### <a name="Def">`Def`</a>: definite

#### Examples

- _ο σκύλος_ "_the_ dog"
- _η γάτα_ "_the_ cat"
- _το πουλί_ "_the_ bird"
