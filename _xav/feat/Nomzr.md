`Nmzr` is a morpheme that transformes another part of speech to a noun. 

### <a name="Ag">`Ag`</a>: Agentive

`Yes` is

#### Examples

* _Wahã dawi robʔré<b>ʔwa</b>&nbsp;_ “I am (someone) who shuts people up..”
