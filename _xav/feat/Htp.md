`Htp` 

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

Heterophoricity is expressed by particles _`ma`_ (indicates the event taking place in the past) and _`te`_ (the action happens in the present).

#### Examples

* _Aibõ, <b>ma</b> wei wi._&nbsp; “A man came.”
* _Aibö <b>te</b> mo buru u._&nbsp; “A man goes to the fields.”
