`Ego` 

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

Egophoricity is marked by a particle _`wa`_.

#### Examples

* _Ĩhe, <b>wa</b> romhu_&nbsp; “Yes I work.”
* _Ĩhe, <b>wa</b> norĩhã wa wapari dza'ra ni._&nbsp; “Yes, we heard.”
