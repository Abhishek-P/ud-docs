`Int` indicates interrogation and is defined in Xavante as the particle _`-e`_.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes


#### Examples

* _<b>E</b> marĩ dzô ma wei wi?_&nbsp; “What did he come for?”
* _<b>E</b> te dza apö mo?_&nbsp; “Will he come back?”
