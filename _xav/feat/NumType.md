`Ego` 

### <a name="Dist">`Dist`</a>:  Distributive Numeral

Distributed numeral marker is expressed by a particle _`mono`_.

#### Examples

* _Ĩhiñimiza'reseõ tô darob <b>mono</b> bö te höimana dza'ra tô; “Old ignoramuses exist indeed in every village.”
