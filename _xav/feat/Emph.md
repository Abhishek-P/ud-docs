`Emph` intensifies the expression by suggesting a contrast or giving weight to a certain word.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

#### Examples

* _aŋ<b>eʔe</b> aŋ pazaru_&nbsp; “And here is some cashiri.”
