### <a name="Part">`Part`</a>: participle

In Bamana, there are three participles with suffixes -len, -ta, -bali respectively and one converb with a suffix -tɔ. They can function as predicates, secondry predicates and modifiers of noun. 

#### Examples

* _Dɔ́nkili <b>dálen</b> bɛ́._ "A song is sang".
* _Ń yé Fàntá dɔ́nkili <b>dátɔ</b> yé._ "I saw Fanta singing a song".

### <a name="Vnoun">`Vnoun`</a>: verbal noun

Verbal nouns are derived from verbs by using a suffix -li.


#### Example

* _Fàntá ká dɔ́kili <b>dáli</b> ká dí né mà._ "The way Fanta sings appeals to me".
