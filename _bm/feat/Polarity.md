In Bamana, all auxiliaries are marked with the `Polarity` feature.
Main verbs are often also annotated with `Polarity=Pos`.

### <a name="Pos">`Pos`</a>: positive, affirmative

#### Examples

* _O <b>ye</b> o dun._ “This has eaten this.”

### <a name="Neg">`Neg`</a>: negative

#### Examples

* _Hali kelen <b>ma</b> den sòrò a ye._ “One has not got a child.”
