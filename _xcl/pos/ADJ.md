### Definition

Adjectives are words that typically modify nouns and specify their properties or attributes. They may also function as predicates, as in

_<b>Սուրբ</b> է անուն նորա:_ / _<b>Sowrb</b> ē anown nora._ “<b>Holy</b> is his name.”

The `ADJ` tag is reserved for adjectives and ordinal numerals, which the traditional grammar classifies as numerals. See [DET]() for determiners and [NUM]() for numerals.

Although participles have adjectival features they are systematically treated as forms of the verb [VERB]() in the UD_Classical_Armenian-CAVaL.

### Examples

- _երանի_/_erani_ “blessed”
- _լաւ_/_law_ “good”
- _ուրախ_/_owrax_ “glad”
- _լսելի_/_lseli_ “audible”
- _առաջին_/_aṙaǰin_ “first”, _երկրորդ_/_erkrord_ “second”, _չորրորդ_/_čʻorrord_ “forth”

### Declension of adjectives

In Classical Armenian, adjectives are declined like nouns in case and number. As a general rule, an adjective agrees with the modified noun in case and number when it follows the noun, but remains in the nominative singular when it preceds the noun (see Meillet 1962: 39-50).

### Degrees of comparison

Classical Armenian does not have dedicated forms of the comparative and superlative degrees of the adjective. There are however various ways to express these meanings. 

The comparative degree can be expressed by the positive form of an adjective along with _քան_/_kʻan_ plus the etalon of comparison in the definite accusative form. The adjectival suffix _-ագոյն_/_-agoyn_ can express either the intensive or the comparative meaning. An adjective in _-ագոյն_ can express the comparative alone or as part of the comparative construction with _քան_.

The superlative degree can be expressed by compounds with _ամենա-_/_amena-_, cf. _ամենա-բարի_/_amena-bari_ “the best; very good”.

### Examples

- _չար քան զառաջինն_ / _čʻar kʻan z=aṙaǰin=n_ “worse than the first one” (Lk. 11:26)
- _ապա թե ոչ մինչդեռ հեռագոյն իցէ_ / _apa tʻe očʻ minčʻdeṙ heṙagoyn icʻē_ “while the other is yet a great way off” (Lk. 14:32).
- _Զի որդիք աշխարհիս այսորիկ. իմաստնագոյնք են քան զորդիս լուսոյ յազգս իւրեանց:_ / _Zi ordikʻ ašxarhis aysorik: imastnagoynkʻ en kʻan zordis lowsoy yazgs iwreancʻ._ “For the children of this world are in their generation wiser than the children of light.” (Lk. 16:8)
- _զի՞նչ իցէ մեծագոյն քան զիմաստութիւն_ / _zi՞nčʻ icʻē mecagoyn kʻan zimastowtʻiwn_ “what is richer than wisdom?” (Wis. 8:5).
