An adverbial modifier of a word is a (non-clausal) [adverb](xcl-pos/ADV) or adverbial phrase that serves to modify predicate or a modifier word. By contrast, adverbials realized by noun phrases or adpositional phrases are tagged as [obl](). 

~~~ sdparse
Եւ վաղվաղակի յարեաւ աղջիկն : \n And straightway the damsel arose.
advmod(յարեաւ, վաղվաղակի)
advmod(arose, straightway)
~~~
