A passive nominal subject is a noun phrase which is the syntactic
subject of a passive clause.

~~~ sdparse
_Առաքեցաւ Գաբրիէղ հրեշտակ յԱստուածոյ ի քաղաք մի Գաղիղեացւոց:_ / _Aṙakʻecʻaw <b>Gabriēł</b> hreštak yAstowacoy i kʻałakʻ mi Gałiłeacʻwocʻ_ \n “The angel <b>Gabriel</b> was sent from God unto a city of Galilee” (Luke 1:26) .
nsubj:pass(առաքեցաւ, Գաբրիէղ)
nsubj:pass(was-sent, Gabriel)
~~~
