The vocative relation is used to mark a dialogue participant addressed in a text. The relation links the addressee’s name to its host sentence.

~~~ sdparse
Ասեն ց նա ՝ այո Տէր : \n They said to Him , “ Yes , Lord ! ”
vocative(այո, Տէր)
vocative(Yes, Lord)
~~~
