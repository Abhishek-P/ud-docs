In Classical Armenian, possessive is a lexical feature of personal and reflexive pronominal adjectives ([determiners](xcl-pos/DET)). Although enclitic determiners =ս/_=s_, =դ/_=d_, =ն/_=n_ can express possession, they are tagged according to their more common deictic contrastive values with the [Deixis]() feature and not the possessive. Unlike <a href="https://universaldependencies.org/hy/feat/Poss.html">Modern Eastern Armenian</a>, genitive case forms like Պարսից/Parsicʻ “of Persis” are not tagged by the possessive feature.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: it is possessive

The tag marks pronominal adjectives, which express the possessive meaning. 

#### Examples

* possessive personal determiners: իմ/_im_ “my”
* possessive reflexive determiners: իւր/_iwr_ “one’s own”
