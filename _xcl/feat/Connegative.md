Boolean feature of verbs [verbs](xcl-pos/VERB), marking a word as being in connegative form.

In Classical Armenian, negated imperative (prohibitive) mood is expressed by a dedicated form of negated imperative and a negative particle մի/_mi_ “not”.
Note that there is no `No` value. If the word is not connegative, the `Connegative` feature will not appear.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: word is connegative

#### Examples

* Prohibitive: մի լար / _mi lar_ “don't cry!”
