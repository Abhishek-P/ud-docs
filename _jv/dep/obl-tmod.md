Example of the used of deprel [obl:tmod]() in Javanese:

~~~ sdparse
Pesawat punika dipunsertifikasi perdana taun 1994 . \n The aircraft was first certified in 1994 .
obl:tmod(dipunsertifikasi, taun)
nummod(taun, 1994-6)
obl:tmod(certified, 1994-15)
~~~
