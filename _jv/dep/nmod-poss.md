Examples of the used of deprel [nmod:poss]() in Javanese:

~~~ sdparse
omah Budi \n Budi 's house
nmod:poss(omah, Budi-2)
nmod:poss(house, Budi-4)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Jeneng é \n her name
nmod:poss(Jeneng, é)
nmod:poss(name, her)
~~~
