Language-specific feature identifying clitics attached to the word.

Komi-Zyrian has a number of particle clitics used to express questions,
politeness, or focus.

### <a name="A">`A`</a>: -а

Expresses question _do you think_.

#### Examples

* [kpv] _Тун кӧ тайӧ нывбабаыс-<b>а</b>?_ "If this woman is a witch, <b>you think</b>?"

### <a name="Add">`Add`</a>: -а

Is an additive expressing focus _too/also_.

#### Examples

* [kpv] _Петас, вокъяс, шонді-<b>й</b> тан!_ "Hey brothers, the sun is going to come out here, <b>too</b>!

### <a name="O">`O`</a>: -ӧ

Expresses question _whether_.

#### Examples

* [kpv] _тӧдмавны, оз<b>-ӧ</b> дзебсясь сэні враг._ "find out, <b>whether</b> there aren't any enimies hiding there"

### <a name="So">`So`</a>: -сӧ

Draws attention to something visible.

#### Examples

* [kpv] _ тадз<b>сӧ</b>..._ "in this way <b>here</b>"

### <a name="To">`To`</a>: -тӧ

Draws attention to mutual knowledge.

#### Examples

* [kpv] _Ме ог зэв вӧлі пукси шойтчыныяс<b>тӧ</b>_ "I was not the kind who would sit down and rest <b>you see</b>"
