As the abessive case, which correlates with English _without_ has been
brought together under one name, and Komi attests to two separate formatives,
a variant feature has been added with the hope of clarification according to
range of usage.


### <a name="Vclause">`Vclause`</a>: -тӧг

The first abessive case (-тӧг) is limited in range to the verbal clause.

#### Examples

- _<b>батьтӧгыс</b> локтіс_ &nbsp;“he/she came <b>without his/her father</b>”
- _Мортыс колис <b>документъястӧг</b>_ “That person was left <b>without documents</b>.”


### <a name="Nomin">`Nomin`</a>: -тӧм

The second abessive case (-тӧм) is limited to the nominal phrase and non-verbal clause.

#### Examples

- _<b>документъястӧм</b> морт сюрис_ “the person <b>without documents</b> was found.”
- _Мортыс <b>документъястӧм</b>_ “That person is <b>without documents</b>/ has <b>no documents</b>.”
