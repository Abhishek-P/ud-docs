A *clausal subject* (`csubj`) is a clause that acts as the subject of
another clause.

<!-- fname:csubj.pdf -->
~~~ sdparse
Пукавны вадорын водзӧ вӧлі весьшӧрӧ. \n Sitting at water's edge was in vain.
csubj(весьшӧрӧ, Пукавны)

~~~
