A numeric modifier of a noun is any number phrase that serves to
modify the meaning of the noun with a quantity.

~~~ sdparse
PRO1 SITTA I@b ÅTTA ÅR TID-FRAMÅT \n I sat for eight years
nummod(ÅR, ÅTTA)
~~~
