An auxiliary of a clause is a verb that expresses temporal/aspectual or modal properties of the
clause.

~~~ sdparse
PRO1 PERF MED.VARA OS@b VM@b \n I have participated in the Olympics and the World Championship
aux(MED.VARA, PERF)
~~~
