Boolean feature of postpositions or adverbs, signaling that the word is a clitic.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

The postposition *upé* and the adverb *nhuntu* have the clitic variants *pe* and *ntu*, respectively, which attach to a preceding word.

#### Examples

Clitic forms attested by Avila (2021):

* _<b>Amaãntu.</b>&nbsp;_ “I'm just looking.” (*a-maã=ntu* 1sg-see-only)
* _<b>kupixá-pe</b>&nbsp;_ “in the plantation” (*kupixá=pe* plantation-LOC)
