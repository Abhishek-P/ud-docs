This feature marks gender agreement of the verb with the indirect object or of postposition with its noun.

### <a name="Masc">`Masc`</a>: Masculine

#### Examples

* [ab] _<b>у-ҽ</b>-аа-ны-кыл(-ø)_ [<b>Refl.2SgM-Refl</b>-Mod-Pv-restrain-Impv] ‘restrain <b>yourself.Sg</b>!’ `Dyn=Yes|Gender[lo]=Neut|Gender[refl]=Masc|Mood=Imp|Number[lo]=Sing|Number[refl]=Sing|Person[lo]=3|Person[refl]=2|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin`

### <a name="Fem">`Fem`</a>: Feminine

#### Examples

* [ab] _<b>б-ҽ</b>-аа-ны-кыл(-ø)_ [<b>Refl.2SgM-Refl</b>-Mod-Pv-restrain-Impv] ‘restrain <b>yourself.Sg</b>!’ `Dyn=Yes|Gender[lo]=Neut|Gender[refl]=Fem|Mood=Imp|Number[lo]=Sing|Number[refl]=Sing|Person[lo]=3|Person[refl]=2|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin`

### <a name="Neut">`Neut`</a>: Neuter = Non-human

#### Examples

* [ab] _а-ҽ-а-ҟье-ит_ [Refl.3SgNH-Refl-Subj.3SgNH-persist-Aor] ‘it persisted’ `Dyn=Yes|Gender[refl]=Neut|Gender[subj]=Neut|Number[refl]=Sing|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[refl]=3|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Aor|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin`
