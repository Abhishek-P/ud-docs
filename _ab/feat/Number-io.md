#### Examples

* [ab] _ø-<b>с</b>-аҳа-хье.ит_ [Subj.3-IO.1Sg-hear-Perfect] ‘I have heard it’ `Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Neut|Number[io]=Sing|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[io]=1|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Prf|Trans=No|VerbForm=Fin`
