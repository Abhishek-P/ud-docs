### Description

Auxiliary verbs are a closed subset of verbs. The following cases are common in
Uralic languages:

* Negation verbs
* Copula
* The verbs used in verb chain constructions like past perfect, if applicable
* Some modal verbs

#### Examples

* [fi] _<b>on</b> tullut_ "<b>has</b> come", _<b>olisi</b> vienyt_
  "<b>would’ve</b> taken"
* [fi] _<b>pitää</b> juosta_ "<b>must</b> run"
* [fi] _<b>en</b> tiedä_ "I do <b>not</b> know"
