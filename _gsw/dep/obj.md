title: 'dobj'
shortdef: 'direct object'
---

`obj` marks a noun phrase that is the direct object of a verb. The direct object is in accusative case.

~~~ sdparse
Die Frau gibt dem Kind einen Apfel . \n The woman gives the child an apple .
obj(gibt, Apfel)
~~~
