### Definition

Nouns (i.e. common nouns) are a part of speech typically denoting a person, place, thing,
animal or idea.

### Examples

- _pige_ "girl"
- _kat_ "cat"
- _træ_ "tree"
- _luft_ "air"
- _skønhed_ "beauty"
