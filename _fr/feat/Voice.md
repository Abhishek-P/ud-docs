Voice on French is used only on Verbs for marking the active-passive distinction.

### <a name="Pass">`Pass`</a>: passive or patient-focus voice

The subject of the verb is affected by the action (patient). The doer
(agent) is either unexpressed or it appears as an oblique dependent
or an object of the verb.

#### Examples

* _Dans quel cas Angiox est-il <b>utilisé</b> ?_ “What is Angiox <b>used</b> for?”

### <a name="Act">`Act`</a>: active voice

The subject of the verb is the doer of the action (agent), the object is affected by the action (patient).

#### Examples

* _Ils ont <b>réalisé</b> un excellent travail._ “They did an excellent job."
