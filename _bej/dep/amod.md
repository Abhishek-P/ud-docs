An adjectival modifier of a nominal is any adjectival phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the nominal head.

~~~ sdparse
mʔ -aːna di -ja waliːk -a =b =u =it / sagi haːʃ =iː // beːn / ʔarjab =wa mariː =b i= dar =iː /
text_en: So, he was calling (others), saying "Come!", from far away in the direction of Aryab.
amod(haːʃ, sagi) ("land", "far")
~~~
