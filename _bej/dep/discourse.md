This is used for interjections and other discourse particles and elements, which are not clearly linked to the structure of the sentence except in an expressive way.
~~~ sdparse
tak / ʔeːgirim // ʔajaːj tak -i iː- fi =t amsi iraːnaj rh -i / oː= kina /
text_en: "There was an old man of my family and, gosh! I even saw him today.
discourse(rh, iraːnaj) ("see", "gosh")
~~~
