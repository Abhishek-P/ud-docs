An adverbial modifier of a word is a (non-clausal) adverb or adverbial phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the word.

~~~ sdparse
tak / ʔeːgirim // ʔajaːj tak -i iː- fi =t amsi iraːnaj rh -i / oː= kina /
text_en: "There was an old man of my family and, gosh! I even saw him today."
advmod(rh, amsi) ("see", "today")
~~~
