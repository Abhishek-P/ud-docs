A copula is the relation between the complement of a copular verb and the copular verb.
For more on the `cop` relation, see the universal dependency description ([u-dep/cop]())
Verbal copulas are tagged [AUX](), not `VERB`.

~~~ sdparse
hoː =b hoːsoː ʃʔag -aː =b =u uː= tak // ʔasal -aː =b iː- kti =jeːb /
text_en: "The man was carrying on his shoulder a lamb that he had grilled.
cop(ʃʔag, =u) ("carry_on_shoulder", `AUX`)
~~~
