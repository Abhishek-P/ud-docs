### Definition

Adverbs are words that typically modify [verbs](VERB) or [nouns](NOUN).
We consider ideophones as adverbs.

### Examples

- _<b>diːseːt</b> ʔit_ "<b>slowly</b> fall asleep"
- _<b>kak</b> jʔ -aː ?_ "<b>how</b> have you come ?"
