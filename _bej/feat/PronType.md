This feature typically applies to [pronouns](pos/PRON), [adverbs](pos/ADV), and relative pronoun [SCONJ](pos/NUM) (relativizer).
.


### <a name="Int">`Int`</a>: interrogative pronoun, adverb

Used to specify the interrogative quality of a [pronouns](pos/PRON) or pronominal [adverbs](pos/ADV).

#### Examples: 
- _kak_ "how"

### <a name="Rel">`Rel`</a>: relative pronoun, subordinating conjunctions

Subordinating conjunctions holding the position of relativizer are describe by this feature.

#### Examples: 
- _bak tʔi <b>-it</b> =eːt ʔiːbaːb / dh =eː ti- ki_ "a story like that happened to me"


### <a name="Dem">`Dem`</a>: demonstrative determiner

Used to show the thing or person being referred to.

#### Examples
- _<b>uːn<b> uː= tak_ "this man"
