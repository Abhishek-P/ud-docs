In Beja, the `Definite` feature is marked only on the [determiners](_bej/pos/DET).

### <a name="Ind">`Ind`</a>: indefinite

#### Examples

* _<b>i=</b> kaːm_ "the camel"

### <a name="Def">`Def`</a>: definite

#### Examples

* _hoː <b>=b</b>_ "a lamb"
