Deixis is typically a feature of demonstrative determiners in Beja. 
Its value classifies the location of the referred entity with respect to the location of the speaker or of the hearer. 

### <a name="Remt">`Remt`</a>: remote, distal

The entity is far away from the reference point (e.g., from the speaker).

#### Examples
- _eːjam <b>baliːb</b> naːjd uːmeːk jʔiːhoːb̚ /_ "When the donkey comes to that water place"

### <a name="Prox">`Prox`</a>: Proximal

The entity is close to the reference point (e.g., to the speaker).


#### Examples
- _<b>eːn</b> miːmaʃaːj geːbi hireːrijeːbu /_ "when I walk by these tombs"
