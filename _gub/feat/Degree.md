`Degree`

### <a name="Dim">`Dim`</a>: Diminutive

The diminutive is expressed with the noun modifier *a'i*, always bound to its host.

#### Examples

* _<b>Pixik-a'i</b> a'e taw&nbsp;_ “That village is very small” (small-DIM PRON village)
