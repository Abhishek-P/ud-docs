`VerbForm`

### <a name="Ger">`Ger`</a>: Gerund

In Guajajara the gerund marker is the posposition pà. The verb in gerund takes the possessive personal forms. 

#### Examples

* _<b>i-pɨhɨk pà</b>&nbsp;_ "to catch it” (NCont-catch GER)
