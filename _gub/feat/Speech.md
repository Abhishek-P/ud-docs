`Speech`

### <a name="Fem">`Fem`</a>: Female 

Female speech is expressed with the sentence-final particle "ʔɨ"

#### Examples


* _<b>Heakagahɨ ʔɨ</b>&nbsp;_ “My head hurts” (Woman speaking)

### <a name="FF">`FF`</a>: Female to female 

Female to female speech is expressed with the sentence final particle "kɨn"

#### Examples


* _<b>Ikatu nezewe kɨn</b>&nbsp;_ “That's ok” (Mother to daughter)


### <a name="Masc">`Masc`</a>: Masculine

Masculine speech is expressed with the sentence final particle "pa"

#### Examples

* _<b>Omowok xu aipo pa</b>&nbsp;_ “Um espinho rasgou-a” (Man speaking)


### <a name="MM">`MM`</a>: Male to male

Male to male speech is expressed with the sentence final particle "tɨ"

#### Examples

* _<b>Zanekaruk tɨ</b>&nbsp;_ “Good afternoon friends”
