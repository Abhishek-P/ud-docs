`Voice` in Guajajara may take the the following values ...

### <a name="Antip">`Antip`</a>: Antipassive

Antipassive voice is marked by the prefix *puru* on the verb.

#### Examples

* _<b>i-puru-ʔu-wer</b>&nbsp;_ “He/She/They want to eat something” (R2-Antip-eat-Des)


### <a name="Cau">`Cau`</a>: causative

Causatives are marked by the prefix *mu-* on the verb.

#### Examples

* _<b>U-mu-wɨwɨk</b>&nbsp;_ “He/She/They sew” (3-CAUS-sew)
* _<b>u-mu-maw</b>&nbsp;_ “He/She/They cause the end" (3-CAUS-finish)

### <a name="Com">`Com`</a>: comitative

Comitative voice is marked by the interfix *-eru-* on the verb.

#### Examples

* _<b>U-era-ha</b>&nbsp;_ “He/She/They brought with them” (3-COM-go)
