The subtype `discourse:emo` is used in TwittIrish for emoticons, emoji and other pictographic elements. The pictogram should depend on the head of the tweet.

~~~ sdparse
@Irish_Dave1 Go raibh maith agat :)
discourse:emo(raibh, :)) 
~~~
