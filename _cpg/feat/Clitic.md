Boolean feature of [pronouns](u-pos/PRON). It tells whether the pronoun form is a clitic.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: it is clitic

Clitic possessive pronouns deriving from the genitive of personal pronouns are formed using the personal pronouns _(e)γó_ “I” or _(e)ghó_ “you”.

#### Examples

* _fikirsúzis <b>mu</b> ádras tiyá kalaǰí ne deyí ípis ta mi?_ “My mindless man why are you telling me this word?”
* mána deyí, baγərdά <b>či</b>, put’ ísu. “He calls her saying 'where are you mother?'”
