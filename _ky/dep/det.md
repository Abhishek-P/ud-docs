The relation determiner (det) holds between a nominal head and its determiner.


~~~ sdparse
кайсы мезгил . \n which period
det(мезгил, кайсы )
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Бул суроолор . \n These questions
det(суроолор, Бул)
~~~
