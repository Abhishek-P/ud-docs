An adverbial modifier of a word is a (non-clausal) [adverb](ky-pos/ADV)
or adverbial phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the word.

Note that nouns in particular morphological [cases](ky-feat/Case),
or followed by an [adposition](ky-pos/ADP) are marked
using [obl]() even if they function as adverbial modifiers.

~~~ sdparse
дарыгердин чуркоонун пайдасы тууралуу айтканы сюрприз болду. \n It was a surprise when the doctor told me about the benefits of running.
advmod(айтканы, тууралуу )
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Бишкекте футзал боюнча Азиянын тандоо туру өтөт. \n Futsal Asian qualifying round will be held in Bishkek.
advmod(футзал , боюнча)
~~~
