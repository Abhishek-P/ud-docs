title: 'discourse'
shortdef: 'discourse element'
udver: '2'
---

This is used for interjections and other discourse particles and elements
(which are not clearly linked to the structure of the sentence, except in an expressive way).

~~~ sdparse
Макул, кечээки газета бар эле . \n Well, there was yesterday's newspaper
discourse(бар , Макул)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Молодец, окудуң. \n Well done! You must have read it.
discourse(окудуң, Молодец)
~~~
