title: 'nummod'
shortdef: 'numeric modifier'
udver: '2'
---

A numeric modifier of a noun is any number phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the noun with a quantity.

~~~ sdparse
төрт күн \n  three days
nummod(күн, төрт)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
биринчи жумада . \n in the first week
nummod(жумада, биринчи )
~~~


~~~ sdparse
145 сом \n 145 soms
nummod(сом, 145)
~~~
