`Number[psor]` feature captures the possessor's number.

### <a name="Sing">`Sing`</a>: singular possessor

#### Examples

* _китебиң_ "your-SG books" `Number[psor]=Sing|Number=Sing`
* _китептерим_ "my books" `Number[psor]=Sing|Number=Plur`

### <a name="Plur">`Plur`</a>: plural possessor

#### Examples

* _китептериңиз_ "your-PL books" `Number[psor]=Plur|Number=Plur`
* _китебибиз_ "our book" `Number[psor]=Plur|Number=Sing`
