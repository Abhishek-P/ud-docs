TIn Tekó, the attributives require suffixes _`-i`_/_`-dʒi`_ to predicate. 

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

#### Examples

* _tukug<b>i</b>_&nbsp; “She is short.”
* _tawa<b>dʒi</b>_&nbsp; “It's yellow.”
