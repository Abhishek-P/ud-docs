The vocative particle, _<b>a</b>_, precedes a person or thing being addressed directly.

### Example

_<b>a</b>nóibingen_ "<b>O</b> Holy Virgin"

~~~ sdparse
a nóib ingen \n o holy maiden
case:voc(ingen, a)
~~~

_<b>a</b> rómanu_ "Romans!"

~~~ sdparse
a rómanu \n o Romans
case:voc(rómanu, a)
~~~

_<b>a</b>thusu_ "You!"

~~~ sdparse
a thu su \n o you
case:voc(thu, a)
~~~
