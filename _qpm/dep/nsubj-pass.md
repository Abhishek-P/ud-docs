The [nsubj:pass]() dependency (passive nominal subject) is used to express the relation of a noun phrase which is the syntactic subject of a passive verb:

~~~ sdparse
zatvóren je poten 
"the road is closed" 
lit:  closed is road-the   
nsubj:pass(zatvóren, póten)
~~~
