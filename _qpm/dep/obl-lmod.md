The [obl:lmod]() dependency (locative modifier), a sub-type of the [obl]() dependency, is used for a modifier that specifies a location:

~~~ sdparse
plývom faf denízane 
"I swim in the sea"
obl:lmod (plývom, denízane)  
~~~
