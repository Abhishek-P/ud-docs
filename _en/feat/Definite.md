In English, the `Definite` feature is marked only on the [determiners](en-pos/DET) _the_, _a_, and _an_.

### <a name="Ind">`Ind`</a>: indefinite

#### Examples

* _a, an_

### <a name="Def">`Def`</a>: definite

#### Examples

* _the_
