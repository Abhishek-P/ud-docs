title:  'aux:pass'
shortdef : 'passive auxiliary'
udver: '2'
---

A passive auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause which
contains the passive information.

~~~ sdparse
Kennedy has been killed
aux:pass(killed, been)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Kennedy was killed
aux:pass(killed, was)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Kennedy got killed
aux:pass(killed, got)
~~~
