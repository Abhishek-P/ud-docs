A passive auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause which
marks it as passive. See: [English passive construction](../feat/Voice.html#pass-passive)

~~~ sdparse
Kennedy has been killed
aux:pass(killed, been)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Kennedy was killed
aux:pass(killed, was)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Kennedy got killed
aux:pass(killed, got)
~~~
