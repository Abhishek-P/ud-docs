title: 'advmod:deg'
shortdef: 'advmod with degree modality'
udver: '2'
---

The _advmod:deg_ relation is used for adverbal modifiers of degree.

~~~ sdparse
..., дзик ас кежас,... \n ..., in their very midst, ...
advmod:deg(ас, дзик)

~~~
