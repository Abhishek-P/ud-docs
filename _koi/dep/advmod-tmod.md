title: 'advmod:tmod'
shortdef: 'advmod with temporal modality'
udver: '2'
---

The _advmod:tmod_ relation is used for adverbal modifiers with temporal modality.

~~~ sdparse
и сийӧ вайисӧ тӧн. \n and they brought it yesterday.
advmod:tmod(вайисӧ, тӧн)

~~~
