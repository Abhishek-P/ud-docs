---
layout: relation
title:  'nmod:poss'
shortdef : 'possessive nominal modifier'
udver: '2'
---

The relation `nmod:poss` is used for a genitive/possessive nominal modifier, expressed either by a nominal or pronoun in the genitive.

~~~ sdparse
Петялӧн ордчӧн олісьыс краситӧм йӧрсӧ. \n Petja's neighbor painted the fence.
nmod:poss(олісьыс, Петялӧн)
~~~
