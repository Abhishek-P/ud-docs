layout: relation
title: 'xcomp'
shortdef: 'open clausal complement'
udver: '2'
---

The xcomp relation is used for clausal complements of verbs which function like objects.

~~~ sdparse
Anh ấy cho bò ăn.
compound:pron(Anh, ấy)
nsubj(cho, anh)
obj(cho, bò)
xcomp(cho, ăn)
punct(cho, .)
~~~
