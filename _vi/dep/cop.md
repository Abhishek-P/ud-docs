A copula is the relation between the complement of a copular verb and the copular verb. Copular
verb in Vietnamese is "là".

~~~ sdparse
Lan là sinh_viên 。
nsubj(sinh_viên, Lan)
cop(sinh_viên, là)
punct(sinh_viên, 。)
~~~
