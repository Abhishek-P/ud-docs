acl:subj is used for the verb is nominalized by a classifier noun . For example ``cái'', ``sự'', ``việc'', ...

~~~ sdparse
Cái ăn khan hiếm quá! 
acl:tonp(cái, ăn)
nsubj(khan hiếm, Cái)
punct(khan hiếm, 。)
advmod(khan hiếm, quá)
~~~
