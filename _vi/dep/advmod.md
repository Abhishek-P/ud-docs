title: 'advmod'

---

An adverbial modiﬁer of a word is a (non-clausal) adverb or adverbial phrase (ADVP) that serves to modify the meaning of the word.

~~~ sdparse
Cô ấy đẹp quá 。
advmod(đẹp, quá)
det:pmod(Cô, ấy)
nsubj(đẹp, Cô)
punct(đẹp, 。)
~~~
