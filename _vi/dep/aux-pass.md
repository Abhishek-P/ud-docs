title: 'aux:pass'
shortdef: 'passive auxiliary'
udver: '2'
---

A passive auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause which contains the passive information. In Vietnamese, some passive verbs are "bị", "được", ...

~~~ sdparse
Tôi bị đánh 。
nsubj(đánh, Tôi)
aux:pass(đánh, bị)
punct(đánh, 。)
~~~
