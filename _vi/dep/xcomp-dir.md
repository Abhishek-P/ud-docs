---
layout: relation
title: 'xcomp:dir'
shortdef: 'open clausal complement for adjective'
udver: '2'
---

The xcomp:adj relation is used for clausal complements of adjective which function like objects.

~~~ sdparse
Anh ấy lấy sách ra.
compound:pron(Anh, ấy)
nsubj(lấy, anh)
xcomp:dir(lấy, ra)
obj(lấy, sách)
punct(sách,.)
~~~
