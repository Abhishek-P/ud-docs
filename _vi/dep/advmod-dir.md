Relation advmod:dir is used for adverbial modifier used to indicate direction.

~~~ sdparse
Nam lấy cơm ra 。
advmod:dir(lấy, ra)
obj(lấy, cơm)
nsubj(lấy, Nam)
punct(lấy, 。)
~~~
