title: 'csubj:pass'
shortdef: 'passive clause subject'
udver: '2'
---

A passive clause subject is a noun phrase which is the syntactic subject of a passive clause.

~~~ sdparse
Nó bị đánh .
csubj:pass(đánh, Nó)
punct(đánh, 。)
aux:pass(đánh, bị)
~~~
