Gender is a inherent feature of nouns and some pronouns
and an inflectional feature of adjectives, other pronouns and some forms of verbs
(participles and third person of past tense) that mark agreement with nouns.
There are two values of gender: masculine and feminine.

### <a name="Masc">`Masc`</a>: masculine gender

Nouns denoting male persons are masculine. Other nouns may be also
grammatically masculine, without any relation to sex.

#### Examples
* هلک _halë́k_ boy
* کور _kor_ house
* ملګری _malgë́ray_ friend

### <a name="Fem">`Fem`</a>: feminine gender

Nouns denoting female persons are feminine. Other nouns may be also
grammatically feminine, without any relation to sex.

#### Examples
* پېغله _péġla_ girl
* مياشت _myấšt_ month
* سپوږمۍ _spoǧmë́y_ moon
