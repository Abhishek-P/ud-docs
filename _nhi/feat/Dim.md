`Dim` can indicate smallness, reverence, and/or familiarity of a NOUN/ADJ.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes


#### Examples

* _notskwin<b>tsitsin</b>_ “My little/lovey dogs.”
* _yeh moawi<b>tsin</b>_ “She is your aunt (reverence).”
