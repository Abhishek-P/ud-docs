Contrastive negation is used in Erzya to negate clauses and phrases, it can occur in virtually all statements and commands:

### <a name="Contrastive">`Contrastive`</a>: contrastive negation

Note that there is no `NonContrastive` value. If the word is not Contrastive, the
`NegationType` feature will just not be mentioned in the `FEAT`
column. (Which means that empty value has the `No` meaning.)

#### Examples

* [myv]_<b>Аволь</b> авардть, тейть мезеяк!&nbsp;_ “<b>Don't</b> cry, do something!”
* [myv]_Иля аварде!_ &nbsp;_ “Don't cry!" 
* [myv]_<b>Аволь</b> морась, пижнесь пиже-ожо вайгельсэ.&nbsp;_ “He did<b>n't</b> sing, he screamed bloody murder.”
* [myv]_Эзь мора концертсэ_ &nbsp;_ “he didn't sing at the concert."
* [myv]_<b>Аволь</b> эзь мора, пижнесь пиже-ожо вайгельсэ.&nbsp;_ “It isn't that he did<b>n't</b> sing, he screamed bloody murder.”
* [myv]_Эзь мора, пижнесь пиже-ожо вайгельсэ.&nbsp;_ “He did<b>n't</b> sing, he screamed bloody murder.”
