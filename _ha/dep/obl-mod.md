The `obl:mod` relation for nominal adjuncts.
They can be (but are not always) introduced by a preposition.

~~~ sdparse
yanàː dà yâːraː nèː kàmankà  //  \n  He has children like you.
obl:mod (yâːraː, kàmankà)
~~~
