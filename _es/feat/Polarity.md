### <a name="Neg">`Neg`</a>: negative

In the UD Spanish treebanks, "no" is tagged as ADV with the feature Polarity=Neg when it is used to negate a verb, an adjective, an adverb, or a sentence- When it is used to respond negatively to a question or request, it is tagged as ADV or PART without the Polarity=Neg feature depending the treebank.

#### Examples

* _Ella no es alta._ "She is not tall."
* _No siempre llueve aquí._ "It does not always rain here."
