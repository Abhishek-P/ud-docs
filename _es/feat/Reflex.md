Reflexiveness is a lexical feature of some [pronouns](PRON) and [determiners](DET). It tells whether the word is reflexive,
i.e. refers to the subject of its clause.


### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: it is reflexive

In Spanish, reflexive pronouns are used when the subject of the verb is doing the action to itself. Reflexive pronouns in Spanish include: "me", "te", "se", "nos", "os", "se".

#### Examples

* _Yo <b>me</b> lavo._ "I wash myself."
* _Él <b>se</b> viste._ "He dresses himself."
* _Nosotros <b>nos</b> vemos en el espejo._ "We see ourselves in the mirror."
