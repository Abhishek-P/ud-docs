title: 'aux:pass'
shortdef: 'passive auxiliary'
udver: '2'
---

The relation `aux:pass` is used to attach a passive auxiliary to the participle of the lexical verb.

~~~ sdparse
Kennedy fue asesinado . \n Kennedy was murdered .
aux:pass(asesinado, fue)
~~~
