title: 'nummod'

A numeric modifier of a noun is any number phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the noun with a quantity.

~~~ sdparse
hay dos espacios \n There are two spaces
nummod(espacios, dos)
~~~
~~~ sdparse
15 años \n 15 years
nummod(años, 15)
~~~
