In Kʼicheʼ, `Mood` is a feature of [finite](VerbForm) [verbs](quc-pos/Verb) and auxiliaries. 

### <a name="Imp">`Imp`</a>: imperative

The speaker uses imperative to order or ask the addressee to do the
action of the verb.

#### Examples

* _<b>Jabʼij</b> che ri atat._ "Anda a decírselo a tu papá."

### <a name="Irr">`Irr`</a>: irrealis

Denotes an action not known to have happened.

#### Examples

* _Xaq <b>ta</b> ne kimbʼe iwukʼ._ "Que fuera yo con ustedes."
