In Kʼicheʼ adjectives can take a suffix, *-alaj* to mark intensification.

### <a name="Ints">`Ints`</a>: intensified

#### Examples

* [quc] _Jun <b>nimalaj</b> tzʼiʼ_ "Un perro muy grande"
