Used for the inner relation in *bahuvrīhi* compounds with nominal first members.

~~~ sdparse
nmod:pred(vapus-, gó-) \n with-the-beauty of-a-cow
~~~

~~~ sdparse
nmod:pred(índra-, śatru-} \n whose-enemy Indra},
~~~
