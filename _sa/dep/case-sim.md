Used for the noun that expresses the standard in a comparison.

You are blazing pure, like dear Mitra (RV 1.91.3c).
~~~ sdparse
śúcis tvám asi priyás ná mitrás \n blazing you are dear like Mitra

cop(śúcis, asi)
nsubj(śúcis, tvám)
obl(śúcis, mitrás)
case:sim(mitrás, ná)
amod(mitrás, priyás)
~~~
