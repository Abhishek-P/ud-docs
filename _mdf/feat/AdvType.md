### <a name="Deg">`Deg`</a>: degree

#### Examples

* _<b>пяк</b> сявор_ “<b>very</b> slowly”

### <a name="Loc">`Loc`</a>: locative

#### Examples

* _и <b>меки</b> мурдайхть._ “..and they turn <b>back</b>.”

### <a name="Man">`Man`</a>: manner

#### Examples

* _пяк <b>вишкста</b> ..._ “quite <b>quickly</b> ...”

### <a name="Mod">`Mod`</a>: modal

#### Examples

* _И, <b>пади</b>, няевонь кеветиешка кизоса цёранякс._ “And, <b>maybe</b>, I looked like a fifteen-year-old boy.”

### <a name="Sta">`Sta`</a>: stative

#### Examples

* _... пси модать лангс <b>кяпе</b> аф шятяват ..._ “... <b>barefoot</b> you can't move around on the hot earth”

### <a name="Tim">`Tim`</a>: temporal

#### Examples

* _Кинди <b>тяни</b> лямбошинкса токан?_ “Who should I go to <b>now</b> for warmth?"
