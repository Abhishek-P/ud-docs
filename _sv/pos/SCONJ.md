### Definition

A subordinating conjunction is a conjunction that links constructions
by making one of them a constituent of the other.

### Examples

- _eftersom_ "because"
- _om_ "if"
- _att_ "that" as in _visa <b>att</b> du kan klara det_ "show that you can do it"
