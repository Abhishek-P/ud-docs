Polarity in Swedish is a feature of adverbs and fixed multiword expressions used to express negation. (The value "Pos" is not used.) 

### <a name="Neg">`Neg`</a>: negative

#### Examples

* [sv] _<b>inte</b>_ "not"
* [sv] _<b>aldrig</b>_ "never"
